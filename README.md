# Sacred Dance Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 2026**

## Overview

Sacred Dance is a reflective consciousness application designed to support self-awareness, journaling, personal reflection, and intuitive exploration.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information and using it responsibly.

---

## Information We Collect

Sacred Dance may store:

- Reflections and journal entries submitted by users
- App preferences and settings
- Anonymous device identifiers used to provide app functionality
- Language preferences
- Profile information voluntarily provided by users
- Usage information required to improve app performance and reliability

---

## How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

- Generate reflections and guidance
- Personalize the user experience
- Improve app functionality
- Provide multilingual support
- Diagnose technical issues
- Improve reliability, performance, and future features

---

## AI Services

Sacred Dance uses artificial intelligence services to generate reflections, guidance, interpretations, and insights based on user inputs.

AI-generated content is intended solely for personal reflection and self-awareness.

Users should not consider app content as medical, psychological, legal, financial, or professional advice.

---

## Data Storage

Sacred Dance uses secure cloud infrastructure, including Supabase, to store and process application data.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.

No system can guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining responsible data protection practices.

---

## Data Sharing

Sacred Dance does not sell personal information.

Information is not shared with third parties except where required to provide app functionality and supporting infrastructure services.

Service providers may process data solely for the purpose of operating and maintaining the application.

---

## User Content

Reflections, journal entries, and other content entered by users remain the property of the user.

Such content is processed only to provide the features and functionality of the application.

---

## Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to provide app functionality, improve the service, comply with legal obligations, and maintain account integrity.

---

## Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting:

**Instagram:** @TheSacredDance

---

## Age Requirement

Sacred Dance is intended for adults aged 18 years and older.

The application is not directed toward individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

---

## International Users

By using Sacred Dance, users understand that information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.
